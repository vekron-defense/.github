## What we work on

VEKRON builds **counter-UAS** systems — drone detection, tracking, and
operator-facing command-and-control — engineered to the bar that
defense procurement requires.

We focus on the four problems that matter to the operator:

- **Multi-modal sensor fusion.** Visual, acoustic, and RF signatures
  combined into a single confident track.
- **Edge inference.** Real-time threat assessment at the perimeter,
  no cloud round-trip.
- **Operator console.** Dense, dark, map-centric. Every decision
  carries a reason. Every action is logged and signed.
- **Fleet management.** Distributed deployments stay coherent across
  unreliable networks.

## Why VEKRON

- **Sovereignty.** Engineered for environments where foreign-cloud
  dependency is unacceptable. Air-gappable from day one.
- **Defense-grade rigor.** Memory-safe core. Full supply-chain
  attestation. Every decision documented; every artifact signed.
- **LATAM theater first.** Built in Brazilian airspace, by Brazilian
  operators, for Brazilian customers and partners — with the export
  posture to support the broader region.
